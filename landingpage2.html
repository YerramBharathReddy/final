Department of Mines and Geology Telangana

Department of Mines and Geology Telangana in the state of Telangana is rich in mineral resources and has a strong mining sector that contributes significantly to its economic growth. The Department of Mines & Geology oversees the exploration, extraction, and management of these resources while ensuring sustainable and environmentally responsible mining practices.

Objectives

OBJECTIVE OF TELANGANA MINING DEPARTMENT

To ensure sustainable, transparent, and scientific mining of minerals for the economic development of Telangana. To regulate mineral extraction responsibly while promoting environmental protection and public welfare. To strengthen mineral governance through digital services, transparency, and efficient permit management.
